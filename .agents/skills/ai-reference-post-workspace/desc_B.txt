Author or edit posts for the ai-reference Jekyll blog (pm430.github.io/ai-reference). Use this skill for ANY request to add, write, draft, rewrite, fix, or cross-link a post here — whether the source is a topic, concept, news or announcement, tool, GitHub repo, YouTube video, or a roundup of articles, and even when phrased casually like "글 하나 더 올리자", "정리 포스트 만들어줘", "이 영상으로 글 써줘", or "다시 써줘", or when the user names a site category (onboarding, skills-mcp, cloud-llm, local-llm, prompt-engineering, use-cases). Always use it instead of writing the post yourself: this blog has exact, easy-to-get-wrong conventions (filename and date format, front matter, the fixed category list, internal post_url cross-links, YouTube embeds) plus a GitHub Pages future-date pitfall that silently breaks the live build, and the skill encodes all of it. Do NOT use it for spinning up a brand-new Jekyll site, editing only _config.yml or layouts, writing a CLAUDE.md or README, explaining how a tag or setting works, or non-blog writing such as LinkedIn or Notion.
